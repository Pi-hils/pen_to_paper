What is Pen to Paper?

There is no "normal" way to grieve.

.
.
.
.

There are no right words to say, there are no right actions to show and there are no gift given that can fill the vacuum left in our hearts when a loved one passes away. There are also no 'correct' way to grieve as everyone grieves differently. This journey to healing the wound we feel opens an opportunity for us to reflect, growth as people, in our faith, and build resilience. This website is created to allow you to express yourself a little differently - through writing. We believe that although, they may not physically be with us, we have left us an invaluable gift, and that gift is the memories we shared with them. Pen to Paper is created to allow you to share these memories through writing ...

  • - a blog
  • - a poem
  • - a song
  • - a verse
  • - a quotes

or share with others some helpful things you do to deal with your loss.



Although gone, the memories remain forever...
